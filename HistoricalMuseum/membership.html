Membership Options

The Schleisingerville to Slinger Historical Museum offers three different kinds of memberships to those who wish to provide continual financial support to the museum. All museum membership options require printing out the required form and mailing it to PO Box 69 Slinger, WI 53086. Memberships may be purchased with cash or by check payable to SSHM.

  • Personal Memberships - Personal Memberships are available on an individual basis for $10 a year or on a familial basis for $20 per year. If you wish to make a continuous donation to the museum, you can purchase a lifetime membership for $250
  • Gift Memberships - Gift Memberships allow you to give membership to a friend or family member and make a contribution to the museum in their name.
  • Business Memberships - Business' that wish to provide financial support can also become members of the museum. All business memberships cost $50 per year and will allow the business to grant up to four people individual memberships.

Our Mission Statement

The Schleisingerville to Slinger Historical Museum will be dedicated to preserving, educating, and promoting our Village's history through the collection and preservation of records, documents, and artifacts.
